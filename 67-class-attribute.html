For a one-year period from November 2010, the Marugame Genichiro-Inokuma Museum of Comtemporary Art (MIMOCA) will host a cycle of four Hiroshi Sugimoto exhibitions.

Each will show the works by the artist thematiaclly contextualized under the heading "Science," "Architecture," "History" and "Religion" so as to present a comprehensive panorama of the artist's oeuvre.

Hours: 10:00 - 18:00 (No admittance after 17:30)
